Who We Are

Soryx is an innovative way to connect teachers and students. We are based in Canada and South Korea. We introduce teachers directly to parents looking for high-quality ESL education for their children. We help teachers and students do well in their classes by using our combined years of teaching experience.

Who We Want

We are seeking English Second Language instructors to join our team. Our ideal candidate will be a native English speaker with experience teaching ESL to children between the ages of 4 and 18. A bachelor's degree and a teaching certificate are preferred. If this sounds like the right fit for you, please contact us.

What We Offer

We offer flexible working hours, competitive salaries, and opportunities to collaborate with like-minded teachers. Teachers can work from home at their own convenience while earning good wages. Our teachers work together to offer high-quality lessons by sharing their experiences and knowledge.
